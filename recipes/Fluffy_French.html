Fluffy French Toast Recipe

Description:

This french toast recipe is a little different because it uses flour. A bit unusual, but many people have liked it better thant he usual french toast they make or have made for them.

This recipe should take 10 minutes of prep, 20 minutes of cook time, and 30 minutes total to have it finished with 12 servings for the whole family at breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Directions:

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
